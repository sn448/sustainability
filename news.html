Environmental news

A lot of the time, the news we hear about the environment is disheartening. It feels overwhelming hearing about all the bad that is happening and it sometimes feels like there is nothing we can do that will make a difference. It is important to hear about the bad news because it raises awareness of the condition of the world, and increases the sense of urgency for change. However, there is still a lot of good news, too! Many people are doing what they can to change the state of the world. From scientists making change on a global scale, to individuals making change locally, these stories are important to tell too! So, on this page, I will be posting some positive news stories about the environment for those environmentalists that need a boost of positivity. Hopefully, sometime in the future, there will be more good news than negative news.
