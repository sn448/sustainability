Environmental news

A lot of the time, the news we hear about the environment is disheartening. It feels like there is nothing we can do to prevent climate change. It is important to have this news circling becuase it raises awareness of the condition of the world, and increases the sense of urgency for change. However, there is still so much good news, too! Many people are doing what they can to change the state of the world. From scientists making change on a global scale, to individuals making change locally, these stories are important to tell too! So, on this page, I will be posting some positive news stories about the world to keep hopes up. Enjoy!
